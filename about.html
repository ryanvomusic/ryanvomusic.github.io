About Me

Singer • Musician • Songwriter

I’m gonna be frank with everyone for a minute.

I really have no idea what I’m doing.

But then again, who does? Who knows what path you'll end up on?

I grew up listening to music anytime I could, searching for songs that matched my every emotion, and being so disheartened when I couldn’t find a song to describe exactly how I felt. It wasn’t until far too late in life that I realized the best way to find that song was to write it myself.

I never even performed in front of anyone — not including my family, a fact that I’m sure they’re all so thankful for — until high school. I owe my entire path in life to the after school program that found me and convinced me to audition for a school musical, because it hooked me.

Now when I need to express a certain emotion, I can write it down and sing my own songs about it. And it makes me so happy that I get to give other people the same feelings I used to get as a kid, finding the exact song to explain how I feel. Now I sing for people all the time. Maybe you’ll catch me singing at a restaurant in LA, although more likely you’ll see me and my friends belting it out way too loud in random spots in the city. Hopefully, you'll even get a little happiness with the songs I’ve been lucky enough to release into the world. Either way, I’m glad music moves you in the way that it moves me.

Music is, and has always been, the best way I’ve found to capture my full range of emotion, heart, and soul, and I’m so lucky to be able to do what I do.
