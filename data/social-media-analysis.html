Social Media Content Analysis

Thorough analysis for small creator in the music industry.

Completed: October 2025 R • SQL • BigQuery • Tableau

Business Task

Analyze data gathered both from social media API and social media insights over a 4 month period to track effectiveness and promotional efficacy. Data was gathered from Instagram, TikTok, and Spotify to track overall performance on social media as well as the translation of view into music streams on Spotify.

Data Sources

Data Sources

Dataset: Social Media Insights (5/1/2025 - 8/2/2025)

Source: Data gathered from Instagra, TikTok, and Spotify APIs as well as user gathered information on posts and Content Creation Effort

Key Variables

  • Interactions/Views/Engagement Rate: Effectiveness of content posted
  • Spotify Streams/Listeners Ability of content to translate into streams
  • Content Creation Effort Calculating ROI
  • Post Day/Post Hour To find times and days where content gathers the most attention

Calculated Variables

  • Streams per Listener Number of streams per each unique individual listener

Methodology

Data Processing and Analysis

Platform: Google BigQuery for data storage and SQL analysis

Data Cleaning: Used R for majority of data cleaning

Analysis Approach: Comparative analysis between social media data and streaming data over time to discover growth and translation of content into streams.

Key Analyses Performed

  • Growth of combined audience over time
  • Comparison of social numbers versus streaming numbers
  • Analysis of TikTok viewer types and Instagram Reach versus Engagement
  • Analysis of Content Creation Effort versus Engagement to track ROI
  • Compared numbers at difference times and days to find the most beneficial times for posting

Visualization

Tableau for interactive dashboard creation and data storytelling

Quality Controls

  • Removed data where some metrics were not being tracked
  • Ensured consistent posting during time period of data gathering

Key Findings

High-Quality Listener Base

  • The average Streams Per Listener (SPL) is 2.67.
  • Social content converts views into high-intent fans on Spotify who listen to multiple tracks.

Low-Effort Content Maximizes ROI

  • Low Effort (Level 1) content drives 21 average total interactions, significantly outperforming High Effort content (13 interactions). However both Low and High Effort content outperform Medium Level content in Engagement Rates.
  • Viral content prefers quick, low-friction content. Time and resources spent on High Effort content are poorly utilized, unless meant to garner high engagement over viral views.

Single "Haunt You" is Stalled

  • "You" and "Haunt You" had a combined 307 total streams in the period. "Haunt You" (released May 2nd) only contributed ≈8% of the total streams for the period, despite being a recent focus.
  • The promotional launch and content tie-in for "HauntYou" were insufficient to gain traction, even among the existing high-quality audience.

TikTok Slightly Penalizes Syndication

  • Native TikTok posts average 314 views, while Cross-Posted TikTok posts average only 223 views.
  • The 29% drop in views confirms the need for a strategy that emphasizes platform-exclusive content to maximize viral reach.

Strategic Recommendations

Adopt a "Low-Effort, High-Volume" Content Model

  • Maximize engagement ROI and free up creation time.
  • Immediately de-prioritize medium and high effort content. Reallocate the saved creation time to identifying and scaling the format of low effort content. Save high effort content for aesthetic appearances, which perform well on Instagram.
  • Anchor this high-volume content to the peak time: afternoons and weekdays. This ensures the most efficient content is being posted when the audience is most engaged.

Execute a Dedicated "Haunt You" Content Relaunch

  • Convert high-quality listeners (SPL of 2.67) to the new single.
  • Create a dedicated, low effort TikTok trend/challenge using the sound clip from "Haunt You." This must be executed with native TikTok content
  • Promote the new single as the call-to-action for all high-performing content. Content is converting quality, but this must be directed to the correct product.

Implement a Strict "Native Only" Strategy

  • Eliminate the cross-posting view penalty and maximize organic reach.
  • Stop all direct cross-posting to TikTok.
  • All content must be created and posted natively and exclusively to TikTok first. Wait 24 hours, download the video without the watermark, and then syndicate (cross-post) to Instagram. This respects both platform algorithms and optimizes for maximum total views.
