About the EP

If you've ever been in a toxic relationship, you know that the process of getting out of it takes time (and probably a bit more forgiveness than some people deserve). Well, that's what this EP is about. il•lum•in•a takes you through the best and the worst of...well, the worst.


I certainly hope you can't relate, but if you can, hopefully this EP can resonate with you.
